Arcane Focus

An arcane focus is a special item designed to channel the power of arcane spells. A sorcerer, warlock, or wizard can use such an item as a spellcasting focus.

  • Crystal 10 gp
    • Large variety of crystals available including Agate, Amethyst, Aquamarine, Citrine, Diamond, Garnet, Hematite, Jade, Lapiz Lazuli, Malachite, Opal and Rose Quartz
    • Sizes may vary.
  • Orb 20 gp
    • Currently, only standard quartz orbs are available and is limited to Amethyst, Citrine, rose quartz and smoky quartz.
  • Rod 10 gp
    • Simple wooden rod with a brass covering on both the bottom and top of the rod.
  • Staff 5 gp
    • Large wooden staff with runes carved on the sides. It is unclear if the runes are for decoration, or have magical properties.
    • Staff specifically created to channel arcane magic.
  • Wand 10 gp
    • Sanded down wand carved out of wood with a magical component at its core.
    • Wand specifically created to channel arcane magic.

Druidic Focus

In tune with nature, a druid can use nature-based items as a spellcasting focus where a spell might require non-consumable materials or components.

  • Sprig of Mistletoe or Holly 1 gp
    • Just a sprig of mistletoe or holly that can be used by druids.
  • Totem 1 gp
    • Object incorporates feathers, fur, bones, and/or teeth from sacred animals.
  • Wooden Staff 5 gp
    • Simple wooden staff drawn whole from an ancient tree.
    • Can be used to channel druidic or arcane magic..
  • Yew Wand 5 gp
    • Wand made out of yew wood.
    • Can be used to channel druidic or arcane magic..
